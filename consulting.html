Integration & migration

Many organizations face challenges when their software products, built on a single technology platform or stack, fail to cater to customers using different platforms. This limitation often locks out potential users and restricts market reach. Similarly, new products are typically launched with support for only one language, such as English, and one locale, such as the United States. As products mature, it becomes critical to address these pain points by expanding compatibility across multiple platforms, languages, and locales, to meet diverse customer needs and broaden market opportunities.

At Commdel, we solve these challenges by offering comprehensive software migration, porting, and internationalization/localization services. Our services enable clients to focus their core engineering teams on enhancing key product attributes like features, performance benchmarks, security, and usability that drive competitiveness and customer satisfaction.

Meanwhile, we handle the technical effort of ensuring seamless compatibility and accessibility across platforms and languages. To simplify and de-risk these transitions, Commdel utilizes predefined templates and questionnaires to map current product specifications, platforms, and locales to the target environments. Our well-defined re-engineering process ensures smooth migrations, minimizing risks to business operations while achieving optimal outcomes for customers.

Whether it's expanding platform compatibility or making products accessible in multiple languages and locales, Commdel provides the expertise to turn these complex tasks into strategic advantages, helping clients solve customer pain points and unlock new opportunities.
