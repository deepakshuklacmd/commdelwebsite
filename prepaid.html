Prepaid

Commdel’s Paynorm CMS and Paynorm Prepaid Solution together provide a versatile and comprehensive approach to card and payment management. Here's how they solve critical business needs and enhance customer value

Paynorm CMS (Card Management System):
  • Comprehensive Solution: Designed for banks, financial institutions, processors, and retailers to issue cards and manage electronic payments efficiently.
  • End-to-End Capabilities: Supports all aspects of card and merchant management, including payment authorization, clearing, and settlement.
  • Seamless Payment Ecosystem: Provides robust support for managing transactions across a variety of use cases.
Paynorm Prepaid Solution:
  • Complete Prepaid Functionality: Delivers end-to-end prepaid capabilities while offering flexibility to work with third-party vendors for seamless integration.
  • Rapid Service Creation: Enables the rapid development of unique, innovative prepaid services tailored to diverse customer needs.
  • Sophisticated Customer Models: Supports advanced configurations such as families, hybrid customers, and multi-tiered hierarchies for corporate clients.
  • Centralized Management: The Prepaid Engine acts as the core hub, handling storage, processing, and program management to ensure efficiency and reliability.

These systems empower businesses to improve operational efficiency, deliver targeted solutions, and scale effectively, meeting both customer expectations and market demands. Also include the functiion, technical features from existing web site.
